Tinola

Description

Tinola is a Filipino soup usually served as a main entr√©e with white rice. Traditionally, this dish is cooked with chicken, wedges of chayote, and leaves of the siling labuyo chili pepper in broth flavored with ginger, onions and fish sauce.
I usually crave this dish the day after a night of drinking.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Chicken legs
  • Neutral cooking oil
  • Sliced ginger
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Fish sauce
  • Water
  • Lemongrass
  • Chayote
  • Red chili pepper leaves

Steps

  1. Heat 1 Tbsp of cooking oil in a wok in medium to high heat.
  2. Add the sliced ginger and cook until lightly roasted.
  3. Add the chopped onions and cook for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 10 seconds.
  5. Add the chicken, 1 tsp salt, and some ground black pepper
  6. Mix thoroughly and continue to cook until chicken has slightly browned.
  7. Add 3 Tbsp fish sauce, render the fat, and cook until the fish sauce is reduced.
  8. Add 2 cups of water, enough to submerge the meat.
  9. Add lemongrass. Wait for it to boil and then put the stove in low heat.
  10. Cover the wok and let it simmer for 40 minutes.
  11. Add the sliced chayote and cook for another 5 minutes.
  12. Add the red chili pepper leaves and continue to cook for another 30 seconds.
  13. Turn off the flame and serve with white rice!
