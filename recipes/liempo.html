Liempo

Description

Liempo means pork belly in Tagalog. In Filipino cuisine, liempo is marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, crushed garlic, calamansi juice, ground black pepper, and brown sugar before being grilled. It is then served with a vinegar-based sauce and rice.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • Pork belly
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp calamansi juice
  • 1 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 head crushed garlic
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Basting sauce

  • 1/2 cup neutral cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup banana ketchup
  • 1/2 cup marinade sauce

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a zip loc or a tupperware and marinade in the fridge overnight.
  2. Grill the pork belly. Baste with basting sauce while grilling.
  3. Serve with rice.
